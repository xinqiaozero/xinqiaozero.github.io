议论文

开头

典型性议论文（含有名言）

模板：
Nowadays,there has been growing awareness that+ 名词短语 +plays a/an important/significant role in our daily life.Just as the saying goes,[名言.]

Eg:For this part ,you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay on the saying Beauty of the soul is the essential beauty.（2020 年 9 月作文）

Nowadays, there has been growing awareness that beauty of the soul plays a significant role in our daily life. Just as the saying goes, 「Beauty of the soul is the essential beauty.」

非典型性议论文

模板：
Nowadays,there has been growing awareness that +题目+plays a/an important/significant role in our daily life.So each of us should learn to +题目去掉名词.

Eg:（2019 年 12 月作文）For this part ,you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay on the importance of having a sense of social responsibility.

Nowadays, there has been growing awareness that having a sense of social responsibility plays a significant role in our daily life. So each of us should learn to realize the importance of social responsibility.（30 词左右）

中间部分

现象与自身

模板：
总结句+现象一般情况（背景+作用）+学生自身（背景+怎么做）

Eg:For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay on the importance of having a sense of family responsibility.（2019年12月作文）

总结句：
Everyone has certain roles to take on his or her family.

现象一般情况（背景+作用）：
Parents are expected to ensure their children are well-nourished and well-educated, helping them build a strong body and grea character.

学生自身（背景+怎么做）:
We college students also need to take our responsibility as adult children.Being independent of our parents as early as possible lightens the financial burden on them.Learning to be mature and sensible can prevent us from becoming a constant source of worry to them.Fulfilling our filialduty to best of our abilities allows to enjoy life in midlife years, retirement, and the twilight years.

正反面

模板：
正面+反面+展望

Eg.For this part ,you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay on the importance of motivation and methods in learning.（2019 年 6 月作文）

正面：
Highly motivated students find learning pleasant and rewarding, and therefore maintain a strong desire to acquire new knowledge and skills, and enough courage to meet new challenges.

反面：
Unmotivated students, in contrast, feel like they are forced to learn and get bored easily with this seemingly endless process.

展望：
In addition, proper methods are a contributing factor in increased efficiency and positive learning outcomes. Top students don』t necessarily spend the most amount of time on learning, but more often than not have found the right ways to improve their academic performance.

结尾

典型性议论文【名言】

模板：
To sum up, if we attach importance to+题目，life/society will reward us with satisfaction/health and fulfillment/harmony. This is undoubtedly favorable to the development of both N. and N.（名词可以根据具体语境换）

Eg:For this part ,you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay on the saying Beauty of the soul is the essential beauty.（2020 年 9 月作文）
写法：

To sum up, if we attach importance to beauty of the soul，life will reward us with satisfaction and harmony. This is undoubtedly favorable to the development of both us and others.（30 词左右）

非典型性议论文【短语】

模板：
To sum up, if we make a conscious effort to+题目，life/society will reward us with satisfaction/health and fulfillment/harmony. This is undoubtedly favorable to the development of both N. and N.（名词可以根据具体语境换）

Eg:For this part ,you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay on the importance of having a sense of social responsibility.（2019 年 12 月作文）

To sum up, if we make a conscious effort to have a sense of social responsibility, society will reward us with health and harmony. This is undoubtedly favorable to the development of both us and the society.（30 词左右）
